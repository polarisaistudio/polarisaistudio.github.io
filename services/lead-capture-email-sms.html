Frequently Asked Questions

How does automated lead capture work?

When someone fills out a form on your website, our system automatically captures their information and immediately sends a personalized welcome email and/or SMS. Follow-up messages are sent automatically based on your drip campaign schedule.

Can I send both email and SMS to the same leads?

Yes! You can create multi-channel campaigns that use both email and SMS. This increases engagement rates by 3-5x compared to email alone. You control when each channel is used.

How much does email and SMS marketing automation cost?

Our lead capture and email/SMS automation starts at $800/month, which includes unlimited email sends, setup of capture forms, drip campaigns, and up to 1,000 SMS messages. Additional SMS credits available.

Do I need any technical skills to manage campaigns?

No technical skills required! We set up everything for you and provide an easy-to-use dashboard. You can create campaigns with simple drag-and-drop tools, and we provide templates to get started quickly.

Can I import my existing email list?

Absolutely! We'll help you import your existing contacts and ensure they're properly segmented. We'll also set up re-engagement campaigns for inactive subscribers.

Is SMS marketing legal? What about compliance?

Yes, when done correctly! We ensure full compliance with TCPA, CAN-SPAM, and GDPR. We include proper opt-in mechanisms, unsubscribe options, and help you maintain compliance automatically.
