Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to implement an AI chatbot?

Most AI chatbot implementations take 1-2 weeks from initial consultation to going live. We handle all the technical setup, training, and integration with your existing systems.

Can the AI chatbot integrate with my existing website and CRM?

Yes! Our AI chatbots integrate seamlessly with popular platforms including WordPress, Shopify, Salesforce, HubSpot, and most major CRM systems. We also offer custom API integrations.

What percentage of customer inquiries can the AI handle?

On average, our AI chatbots handle 70-85% of customer inquiries without human intervention. Complex questions are seamlessly routed to your team with full context.

How much does an AI chatbot cost?

Our AI chatbot service starts at $800/month for small businesses. This includes setup, training, ongoing maintenance, and unlimited conversations. Enterprise plans with advanced features start at $2,000/month.

What if the AI doesn't know the answer?

The AI is trained on your business information, FAQs, and policies. If it encounters a question it can't answer confidently, it will gracefully hand off to your team or capture the customer's contact info for follow-up.
