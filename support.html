Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Polaris AI Studio offer?

We offer four core services: AI-powered creative tools and platforms (Polaris Forge), self-published children's books (Books by Polaris), cybersecurity & cloud consulting, and health insurance services with bilingual support (Polaris Benefits).

How do I get support for your mobile apps?

For any app-related questions or technical issues, please email us at info@polarisaistudio.com with your device information, app version, and a description of the issue. We'll respond as quickly as possible.

Do you offer custom AI tool development?

Yes! We provide freelance AI tool design and deployment services. Contact us at info@polarisaistudio.com to discuss your project requirements.

Where can I purchase your children's books?

Our children's books are available on Amazon through our KDP publishing. We offer both digital and print-on-demand editions with bilingual (English-Chinese) options.

What is your privacy policy?

We take your privacy seriously. You can read our complete privacy policy here. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

How can I report a bug or request a feature?

We welcome your feedback! Please send bug reports or feature requests to info@polarisaistudio.com with as much detail as possible. Your input helps us improve our products.
