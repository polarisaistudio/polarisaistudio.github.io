Pricing Questions

What's included in the setup fee?

The setup fee covers discovery calls, custom solution design, AI training on your business data, system integration, testing, and team training. You only pay it once.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. If you upgrade, we'll add the new features. If you downgrade, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

What happens if I go over my conversation limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can either upgrade your plan or pay $0.10 per additional conversation. We never shut off your service without warning.

Do you offer discounts for annual payment?

Yes! Pay annually and save 15% (2 months free). We also offer discounts for nonprofits and startups—just ask during your consultation.

What if the automation doesn't work for my business?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your first month and setup fee—no questions asked. Plus, we work with you to make adjustments before that.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. All plans are month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel. We earn your business every month.

What kind of support do I get?

Starter: Email support (24hr response). Professional: Priority email + monthly calls. Enterprise: Dedicated account manager + 24/7 support. All plans include technical support for the AI system.
